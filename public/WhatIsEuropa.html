What is Europa? Europa, is the smallest of the four Galilean moons orbiting Jupiter, and the sixth-closest to the planet. It is also the sixth-largest moon in the Solar System.

A moon orbiting Jupiter
4.5 billion years old, and 485 million miles from the sun
Smaller than our moon, but bigger than Pluto
Has an ice crust (-210 < temp < -370) surrounding it, very dense
